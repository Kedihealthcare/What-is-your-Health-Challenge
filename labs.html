Common Lab Tests

Find information about various lab tests offered by our facility.

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

A CBC is a blood test used to evaluate your overall health and detect a wide range of disorders, including anemia, infection and leukemia.

Blood Glucose Test

Measures the amount of glucose (sugar) in your blood. It's used to screen for, diagnose, and monitor diabetes.

Lipid Panel

Measures the levels of cholesterol and triglycerides in your blood to assess your risk of cardiovascular disease.

Thyroid Function Test (TFT)

Checks how well your thyroid gland is working by measuring the levels of thyroid hormones in your blood.

For a complete list of tests and detailed information, please contact us.
