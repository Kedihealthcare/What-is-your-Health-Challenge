Understanding KEDI Haemocare: Key Ingredients and Their Action

KEDI Haemocare's effectiveness comes from its blend of crucial vitamins, minerals, and traditional herbs:

  1. Ferrous Gluconate (Iron): An easily absorbed form of iron, vital for hemoglobin formation and oxygen transport, essential in preventing and managing iron-deficiency anemia.
  2. Folic Acid (Vitamin B9): Crucial for red blood cell production and DNA synthesis. Works synergistically with Vitamin B12.
  3. Vitamin B12: Essential for healthy neurological function and plays a critical role in red blood cell maturation.
  4. Radix Astragali (Huang Qi): A powerful adaptogenic herb known to boost energy (Qi), enhance immunity, and support overall vitality, often used in blood-tonifying formulas.
  5. Angelica Sinensis (Dong Quai): Referred to as 'female ginseng,' it's widely used to nourish blood, improve circulation, and balance hormonal health.

Key Benefits of Incorporating KEDI Haemocare

  • Improves Blood Circulation: Promotes better flow of oxygen and nutrients throughout the body.
